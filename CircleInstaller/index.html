CircleOS Installer

Welcome

This installer will guide you through flashing CircleOS to slot B of your device. Your existing Android OS remains untouched on slot A and can be restored at any time.

⚠️ Unlocking the bootloader will wipe all data from your device. Back up everything before continuing.
Requirements: Desktop Chrome or Edge · USB cable · Pixel 6 or Redmi Note 12

Connect Device

Enable USB debugging on your device (Settings → Developer options → USB debugging), connect via USB, then click Connect.

Unlock Bootloader

Your device bootloader must be unlocked to flash a custom OS. This step wipes all data.

⚠️ All data on the device will be erased. This cannot be undone.

Flash CircleOS

Streaming payload.bin from the CDN and flashing to slot B. Do not disconnect the USB cable.

Waiting…

Set Active Slot

Setting the active boot slot to B (CircleOS) and rebooting into your new OS.

Done!

CircleOS is booting on your device. Complete the setup wizard on-device.

To revert at any time: open CircleSettings → Revert to previous OS, or use the Restore Previous OS tab above.

Restore Previous OS

This will switch your device back to slot A (your original Android OS) and reboot. No data is lost — your stock Android picks up exactly where you left it.

Connect your CircleOS device via USB in fastboot mode (hold Power + Volume Down at boot).
